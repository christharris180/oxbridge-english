Who Is This Course For?

Oxbridge English is designed for Spanish-speaking adults who want to learn English in a practical, self-directed way. Whether you are a complete beginner or have some existing knowledge, our method guides you step by step through real communication — without the pressure of a traditional classroom.

This course is ideal for: working professionals preparing for job interviews or international business, university students looking to strengthen their academic English, travellers and those planning to live or study abroad, parents who want to support their children's English education, and anyone who has tried other methods without success.

About Your Teacher

Christopher Harris is a native English teacher with over 15 years of experience teaching English to Spanish-speaking students across Latin America. His communicative method is built on the belief that real language learning happens through meaningful interaction — not memorisation or grammar drills.

The Oxbridge English course brings Christopher's proven classroom method to an online format, with a virtual video guide that walks you through every lesson just as if you were sitting in a real class — but on your schedule, at your pace, and from anywhere in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior English knowledge to enroll?

No. The course is designed to take you from the very beginning, so complete beginners are very welcome. If you already have some knowledge, the structure of the course means you can move through the early stages quickly and focus on the areas where you need the most practice.

How long do I have access to the course?

You get lifetime access. Once you enroll, the course is yours to keep and revisit as many times as you like, with no expiry date.

Can I study on my phone or tablet?

Yes. The Coursify platform is fully compatible with smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. You can switch between devices and your progress is always saved.

Can I study with a group?

Absolutely. The Oxbridge English method works very well in small groups. Many students study together with colleagues, classmates, or family members, which adds a communicative dynamic that accelerates learning.

Will I receive a certificate when I finish?

Not currently. Oxbridge English is focused on practical communication skills rather than formal certification. However, the knowledge and confidence you gain are immediately applicable in real-world situations.

How is this different from apps like Duolingo?

Apps like Duolingo are gamified repetition tools — they are useful for vocabulary but they do not teach you to actually communicate in English. Oxbridge English is a structured course built on communicative methodology, guiding you through real conversations and practical language from the very first lesson.

What payment methods are accepted?

Enrollment is processed securely through the Coursify platform, which accepts PayPal. Payment is straightforward and your personal details are fully protected.
