Frequently Asked Questions

My child has always struggled with English in school. Will this work?

Yes. That is exactly why we designed this as a self-study program. We remove the anxiety, the fear of participating in front of classmates, and the pressure of school grades. They can study in a stress-free environment, making mistakes safely and building real confidence.

How do the mock exams work?

The mock exams are hosted directly on the Hotmart platform as interactive test pages. They mimic the reading formats and question styles of the actual ICFES exam to train your child's reading speed and test-taking strategy.

Are there live classes or tutor check-ins?

No. Once the bundle is purchased on Hotmart, the student has full, immediate access to all courses, books, and exams to study 100% autonomously. We will not interrupt their process, allowing them to fit the preparation around their school schedule.
